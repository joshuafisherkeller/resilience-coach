# Generation prompts

## Shared production prompt

Use case: illustration-story. Asset type: literal picture support for an adult-guided resilience practice application for children ages 6–8. Style: original warm editorial children’s illustration; soft gouache and subtle paper-cut texture; strong clean rounded silhouettes; expressive but grounded faces; simple uncluttered warm-paper background; polished contemporary educational product. Palette: deep teal, dark teal, soft mint, warm paper, warm gold, ochre, gentle coral, muted violet, and sky blue. Composition: the named action is immediately legible at thumbnail size; natural anatomy and hands; ample breathing room; safe crop. Constraints: ordinary non-dangerous setback; emotionally safe; no copyrighted character resemblance; no AI avatar; no written words, letters, labels, numbers, speech bubbles, UI controls, emojis, watermarks, signatures, logos, trophies, streaks, badges, scores, prizes, or confetti. Avoid malformed limbs, duplicate people, floating objects, exaggerated distress, forced happiness, stereotypes, clutter, and clinical imagery.

The exact asset-specific requests used for generation are recorded in the manifest. Reference sheets are used as visual continuity inputs for scenario and card production. Regeneration prompts repeat all character invariants and request only the targeted correction.

## Reference assets

- `style-board`: a six-vignette, text-free board demonstrating palette, gouache grain, paper-cut edges, calm home/learning settings, expressive faces, literal hands, and 128-pixel silhouette clarity.
- `character-reference-sheet`: text-free full-body and three-quarter studies of the three scenario child/grown-up groups, with consistent clothing, proportions, skin tones, hairstyles, and accessibility-conscious variety.
- `expression-reference-sheet`: text-free head-and-shoulder and upper-body studies for frustrated, disappointed, worried, confused, calm-ready, and unsure, with restrained intensity and distinct posture cues.

## Scenario prompts

Sharing: preserve the medium-brown child with short black curls, mustard shirt, teal overalls, and ochre sneakers; the light-skinned peer with wavy auburn hair, sky-blue top, and plum trousers; and the same warm play area, mint rug, low teal shelf, and generic wooden toy vehicle. Sequence: peer has toy → primary child notices tight hands/raised shoulders/warm cheeks → small pause/choice with nearby grown-up → respectful turn request shown through gaze and open-hand gesture without text → child has a next step while the peer may still be playing.

Mistake: preserve the light-tan child with straight dark bob, coral shirt, and teal trousers; medium-brown grown-up in muted violet; same craft table and paper collage. Sequence: child notices torn/misplaced piece → notices mild body clues → pauses and isolates one smaller repair step → tries repair or asks for help → reviews a simple next-time picture plan without text.

Change: preserve the dark-brown child with braids and gold clips, violet hoodie, and ochre trousers; light-brown grown-up with short dark hair and teal cardigan; same rainy window and entry area. Sequence: learns outdoor play moved indoors → notices disappointment/body clues → sees two concrete indoor choices → practices the chosen new plan while disappointment can remain → checks and makes a next-time plan.

## Card prompts

Emotion cards use one face, one posture, and one context cue. Body-clue cards depict the literal clue, with only gentle non-medical overlays for internal sensations. Strategy cards show exactly one bounded action. Grown-up cards show calm co-regulation at the child’s level. Completion images show a simple picture plan or a return to ordinary activity without reward imagery.

## Transparent motif prompt

Create a small original gouache sprout with two rounded leaves and a warm-gold seed on a perfectly flat solid `#FF00FF` chroma-key background. The background is one uniform color with no shadow, gradient, texture, reflection, floor plane, or lighting variation. Keep the sprout fully separated from the background with crisp edges and generous padding. Do not use magenta in the subject. No cast shadow, contact shadow, text, watermark, face, mascot traits, badge shape, or reward symbolism.
